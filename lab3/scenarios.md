# Lab 3: Reward Design Lab
## Scenarios to choose from:

### Scenario: Warehouse Robot

Goal:
Move boxes efficiently

Constraints:
Avoid damage, avoid congestion

### Scenario: Content Recommendation System

Goal:
Keep users engaged

Constraints:
Avoid misinformation, doomscrolling

### Scenario: Self-driving delivery robot

Goal:
Deliver packages quickly

Constraints:
Pedestrian safety, traffic laws

### Scenario: Customer support chatbot

Goal:
Resolve issues efficiently

Constraints:
Accuracy, politeness, escalation when unsure

### Scenario: Energy grid controller

Goal:
Minimize cost

Constraints:
Reliability, fairness, peak demand

### Scenario: Medical triage assistant 

Goal:
Prioritize patients for care efficiently

Constraints:
Avoid missed emergencies, minimize bias, escalate when uncertain
